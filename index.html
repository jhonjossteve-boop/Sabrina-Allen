Core Philosophy

My trading philosophy centers on capital preservation first, growth second. Every position is meticulously sized to ensure no single trade can significantly impact portfolio performance.

Through rigorous backtesting and continuous performance monitoring, I maintain a systematic approach that removes emotion from trading decisions. Risk-adjusted returns matter more than absolute gains.

Market conditions evolve, and so do my strategies. I adapt position sizing, leverage, and asset allocation based on current volatility regimes and macroeconomic factors while maintaining core risk management principles.
